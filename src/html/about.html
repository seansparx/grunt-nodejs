about

mission

To enable the most deep, lasting and rewarding relationships between every person and organisations of all types across the world.

vision

Founded in 2015, glufrag is the world’s challenge online platform. An intuitive, inspiring and fun website aiming to bring people and organisations, in every corner of the world, together. Our passion and focus is to unlock the creative potential of people and organisations and give them the possibility of establishing trustful, loyal and mutually beneficial relationships. We empower organisations to grow their audience, exposure, brand value and sales, while people can win amazing prizes by showing and share their videos, photos and stories about challenges launched by organisations.

values

Five key values define the culture of glufrag: 1) customer‐centric: everything we do is focused on our customers; 2) excellence: we provide a high quality and invaluable service to our customers; 3) inspirational: we inspire people and organisations across the world to launch and participate in challenges; 4) impact‐oriented: our online platform enables a positive and significant impact on people’s lives, organisations andsociety; and 5) ethical: we are firmly against any type of offence and discrimination.

team

We are a group of highly motivated people working in a multicultural and creative environment dedicated to provide an excellent service and positively unforgettable experience to glufrag’s customers.world.
